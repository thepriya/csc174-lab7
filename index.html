Valerie Tam

Valerie Tam is a sophomore at the University of Rochester majoring in data science with clusters in Chinese and Psychology. She is also the marketing director of the Hong Kong Students’ Association. As the marketing director, she updates the club’s social media pages and creates posters for events. She was born and raised in New York City. Some of her favorite things to do in New York City include walking around Soho and going to the best bubble tea shops, such as Bar Pa Tea and Yaya Tea. In addition, she considers herself a foodie, always trying the newest restaurant or dessert shop which is why she created a foodstagram to document all of her food journeys.
